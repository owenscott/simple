A Pretty Simple Website

Most real websites are messy. They are full of scripts, stylesheets, hidden sections, search-engine-optimization (SEO), and all kinds of other miscellaneous HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Looking through the source code of real websites can be intimidating or confusing, even for people who have a lot of experience.

This website is intentionally simple. Hopefuly it's simplicity will make it easier to understand how you can examine a live website.
